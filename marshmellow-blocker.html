Marshmellow Delay

Do you sometimes go on zombie mode when browsing the web?
Marshmellow Delay helps to fight instant gratifaction and therefore break bad habits. Just wait for the first marshmellow to appear, collect it and you can open the page. If you have to invest a little bit of time upfront, it makes sure that your site visit is worth it.
